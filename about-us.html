About Company

Crafting Timeless Spaces that Reflect “The Way We Live”

Artha provides end-to-end interior design services: concept and spatial planning, material and finish curation, bespoke joinery and furniture direction, styling, and design supervision through to handover. When required, the studio collaborates with trusted fit-out and build partners to offer seamless, single-point design and delivery for villas, apartments, and boutique commercial spaces.
